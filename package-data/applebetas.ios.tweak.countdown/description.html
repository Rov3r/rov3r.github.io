Compatible with iOS 7-10

Adds a countdown to your Lock Screen (replacing the "Press home to unlock" or "Slide to unlock" text) to a date of your choosing.

Countdown is highly customizable, letting you change many things about how it displays countdowns, such as the format to use.

Planned:

  • Choosing a custom countdown date component (e.g. month, days, year). You can currently only count down by days.

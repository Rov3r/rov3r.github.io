Compatible with iOS 10

Enable the special watch faces on any Apple Watch.

In watchOS, there are three sets of watchfaces that are included but only shown if you have a certain watch model. These are:

  • Nike+ Digital
  • Nike+ Analog
  • Hermès faces

With this tweak installed, they will show up in the watch app and be able to be added from the Gallery tab.

Important

  • Reminder: Your special (not meant for your model, such as Nike+ or Hermès) watchfaces may disappear at any time and need to be re-added from the Watch app on iOS. I will not provide support if this happens, as this verification is done on the watch side and there is nothing I can do about it.

Known issues

    • Special watchfaces may disappear if Apple Watch is rebooted
    • The Hermès watchface appears with squares rather than numbers
